Book Covertylerchen

When creating a cover for the classic novel, To Kill A Mockingbird I wantted to take some themes from the novel and incoporate them into a powerful yet simple design.



The scene in the book where Atticus prevnts the lynching of Tom is one of the most critical points in the story, and is where I took inspiration for the design. At that point in the novel there is a conflict between life and death, due course and mob justice, rationality and racism, and other contradictory ideas.


I tried to emulate the stark contrast of these themes by highlighting a single hanging noose against a dark background. The minimalistic design and somewhat morbid image of the noose are intended to bring the viewer's attention to the book, without being distracting or overwhelming.
