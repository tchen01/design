Glitch Arttylerchen

"Glitch art is the aestheticization of digital or analog errors, such as artifacts and other "bugs", by corrupting digital code/data"

I'm currently working on a series of glitches of images taken during my most recent trip to Asia. I spent the past summer studying in Hong Kong, visiting relatives in China, and traveling around east asia. All the images were creating using very basic pixel sorting scripts, which I wrote from scratch using the Python Image Library.
